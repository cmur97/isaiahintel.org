About Us

A nonprofit built for digital-age protection.

Isaiah Intel Institute is a Delaware nonprofit corporation dedicated to protecting vulnerable individuals through research, education, technology development, and lawful open-source analysis.

Who we are

Isaiah Intel Institute was formed to help address the growing threat of online sexual exploitation. The organization is focused on prevention, public awareness, victim protection, and responsible technology development.

Our goal is to build a credible, ethical, and effective nonprofit that supports communities, donors, researchers, and organizations committed to protecting vulnerable people online.

Governance

Isaiah Intel Institute is governed by an independent Board of Directors. The organization is committed to ethical governance, financial accountability, and lawful operations.

Our values

We are building with a long-term commitment to integrity, transparency, and public benefit.

Integrity

We aim to operate with honesty, legal compliance, and responsible stewardship.

Protection

We keep vulnerable individuals and victim protection at the center of our mission.

Accountability

We are committed to transparent governance, financial records, and mission-aligned operations.
