Our Mission

Combating online sexual exploitation through lawful research, education, and technology.

Isaiah Intel Institute exists to protect vulnerable individuals by advancing prevention-focused research, public education, technology development, and victim protection initiatives.

What we are building

Our work is designed to support public safety and victim protection by improving understanding of online exploitation risks and developing practical tools for prevention and awareness.

  • Research into online exploitation trends and digital risk patterns.
  • Educational resources for families, churches, schools, communities, and organizations.
  • Technology tools that support lawful analysis and public safety objectives.
  • Public awareness initiatives that help prevent abuse before it occurs.

Mission Statement

Isaiah Intel Institute is dedicated to preventing online sexual exploitation through research, education, lawful open-source intelligence analysis, technology development, and victim protection initiatives.

Our guiding principles

The organization is built around lawful, ethical, and victim-centered work.

Lawful

We emphasize lawful research, public information, ethical data handling, and appropriate collaboration.

Victim-centered

Our work is focused on protecting vulnerable individuals and supporting prevention efforts.

Educational

We believe awareness and prevention are essential to reducing exploitation and protecting communities.
